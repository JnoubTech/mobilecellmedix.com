Everything you need to know about our repair services.

We know how tough it is to be without your phone. That's why we aim to complete most standard repairs—like screen or battery replacements—in under an hour. Drop it off, grab a coffee, and it will usually be ready before you get back.

Absolutely. Quality is our top priority, which is why we back our repair services with a solid warranty. For example, our battery replacements include a full 1-year guarantee, giving you complete peace of mind long after you leave the shop.

Walk-ins are always welcome at our Port Credit location! If you're pressed for time, you can also schedule our mobile repair van to come directly to your home or workplace for ultimate convenience.

Getting a quote is simple and totally free. You can give us a call at (905) 302-1942, shoot us a message, or just swing by the shop. We'll diagnose the issue at no cost and give you an upfront price before any work begins.

Come visit us at 257 Lakeshore Rd E in the heart of Port Credit, Mississauga (ON L5G 1G8). Can't make the drive? Let us know, and our fully-outfitted mobile lab will drive to you.

Most hardware repairs won't affect your data. However, if you want to be extra safe, we offer a dedicated data backup service. We can securely save your information before the repair and restore it immediately afterward.

We like to make things easy. You can pay using all major credit cards, Interac debit, or cash.

If it has a screen and a battery, chances are we can fix it. From shattered iPhone screens and degraded batteries to MacBook logic board issues and broken PlayStation HDMI ports, our expert technicians handle it all.

If your phone is dying quickly, shutting down unexpectedly at 20%, or feeling sluggish, your battery has likely degraded. For iPhones, we generally recommend a replacement once your "Battery Health" drops to 80% or below. A fresh battery will make your device feel brand new again.

Apple uses strict software locks on their components. Even when we use premium, thoroughly tested parts to fix your device, your iPhone may display a notification simply because the repair wasn't done at an Apple Store. Rest assured, this is a standard software notification and has absolutely zero impact on your phone's performance or safety.
